# CLAUDE.md — ai_cop

This repository is a research compendium exploring AI applications in pharma and biotech. The owner has an IT/data background with 9 years in the pharmaceutical industry and is building expertise toward AI-driven drug target discovery.

## Repository purpose

Four research tracks, each grounded in one deep concept:

- `genomics-stats/` — **Uncertainty**: statistical foundations for genomic analysis
- `time-series/` — **Dynamics**: biological processes that change over time
- `signal-processing/` — **Signal integration**: extracting structure from noisy biological data
- `causality-and-targets/` — **Causality**: from genomic association to therapeutic hypothesis

## How to help

When the user asks questions in this repository:

- Connect concepts across tracks whenever possible. Statistics, signal processing, and genomics are not separate subjects here — they are layers of the same problem.
- Prioritize biological intuition over mathematical formalism. The goal is deep understanding, not exam preparation.
- When explaining a method, always answer: "where does this appear in real research or drug discovery?"
- Suggest connections to relevant papers, datasets, or tools when appropriate.
- The user is comfortable with Python and data concepts. No need to explain basic programming.

## Preferred teaching style

- Intuition before formula
- Concrete biological example before abstract definition
- Short answers that invite follow-up questions rather than exhaustive lectures
- When the user seems stuck, offer a simpler reframing rather than more detail

## Long-term goal

The user is building toward: AI-driven genomic research projects, potentially including polygenic risk modeling, target discovery, and multi-omics integration. Everything taught here should connect — directly or indirectly — to that goal.
